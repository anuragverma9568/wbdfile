My Branch

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an engineering course that teaches students how to design, develop, implement, and maintain computer systems, software, and networks.

Some of the core subjects in the CSE syllabus include: Operating systems, Design and analysis of algorithms, Programming languages, Microprocessors, Cryptography, and Network security.
